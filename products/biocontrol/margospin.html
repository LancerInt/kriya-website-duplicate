Mode of Action

The neem oil fraction contributes contact smothering, feeding deterrence, and endocrine di...

The neem oil fraction contributes contact smothering, feeding deterrence, and endocrine disruption that interferes with molting and oviposition, while spinosad acts on nicotinic acetylcholine receptors to induce rapid neuromuscular dysfunction. Together, the formulation suppresses feeding, development, reproduction, and overall pest survival across mixed infestations.
