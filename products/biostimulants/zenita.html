Mode of Action

Bioactive peptides and low-molecular-weight plant compounds are absorbed by foliage a...

Bioactive peptides and low-molecular-weight plant compounds are absorbed by foliage and act as metabolic activators, stimulating enzyme systems, nutrient transport, and root-associated growth responses. This improves nutrient uptake efficiency, supports stress tolerance through osmotic and antioxidant regulation, and promotes faster recovery of active growth.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar programs in fruits, vegetables, and plantations.

  • Greenhouse nutrient programs for hydroponic or substrate systems.

science Compatibility

  • Generally compatible with neutral-pH fertilizers and crop inputs; avoid highly alkaline or strongly acidic concentrates.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Standardized to 40% neem peptide extract.
