Mode of Action

Plant-derived metabolites act as metabolic primers that stimulate enzyme activity, im...

Plant-derived metabolites act as metabolic primers that stimulate enzyme activity, improve nutrient uptake processes, and support root-growth signaling. This enhances plant resilience to abiotic stress, promotes steady vegetative development, and improves physiological efficiency during active growth.

Primary Benefits

Keeps crops vigorous under variable conditions

  • vegetative vigor
  • nutrient uptake enhancement
  • stress tolerance
  • root initiation
  • recovery from growth checks
Target Use & Diseases

Supports crop health and suppresses disorders

  • Helps mitigate physiological disorders linked to nutrient imbalance.
  • Improves plant tolerance to foliar blights when used alongside fungicides.
  • Reduces abiotic stress-induced pest flare-ups by keeping plants vigorous.

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume Application Method

Application Timing Frequency Recommendation
From active vegetative growth onward Repeat every 10-15 days Use at regular intervals to maintain steady metabolic activation.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar sprays for open-field and protected horticulture.
  • Drip or sprinkler fertigation programs for field crops.
  • Nurseries, greenhouse starts, and transplant dips.
  • High-value plantations requiring yield stability.

science Compatibility

  • Generally compatible with neutral-pH fertilizers and crop inputs; avoid highly alkaline or strongly acidic concentrates.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Standardized to 4% plant extract complex.
  • Typical shelf life: 18-24 months in the unopened original pack under cool, dry, shaded storage.
  • Store sealed in a clean, dry place away from heat, direct sunlight, and contamination.
