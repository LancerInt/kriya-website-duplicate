Mode of Action

Seaweed-derived polysaccharides, betaines, and hormone-like compounds activate plant ...

Seaweed-derived polysaccharides, betaines, and hormone-like compounds activate plant metabolism, improve membrane function, and stimulate root growth responses. This enhances nutrient uptake, increases tolerance to drought, salinity, and temperature stress, and supports more stable reproductive development.

Application Timing Frequency Recommendation

science Compatibility

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Standardized to 32% seaweed extract solids.
