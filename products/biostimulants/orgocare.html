Mode of Action

Bioactive plant metabolites act as signaling and metabolic priming molecules that enh...

Bioactive plant metabolites act as signaling and metabolic priming molecules that enhance enzyme activity, nutrient uptake pathways, and antioxidant defense systems. This stimulates root growth responses, improves tolerance to abiotic stress, and supports steadier plant performance during high-demand growth phases.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage
