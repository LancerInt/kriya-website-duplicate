Product Overview

Plant-Derived Surfactant Blend for adjuvant / substrate

Mystica is a plant-derived wetting and spreading agent formulated to improve spray coverage, adhesion, and uptake of foliar applications. By lowering surface tension, Mystica ensures droplets spread uniformly on leaf surfaces, enhancing the effectiveness of biocontrols, fertilizers, and crop protection inputs. The formulation is residue-conscious and compatible with organic programs, giving distributors a premium adjuvant that supports sustainable crop care strategies.

Mode of Action

Mystica reduces the surface tension of spray solutions, causing droplets...

Mystica reduces the surface tension of spray solutions, causing droplets to spread into a thin, uniform film on contact with leaf surfaces rather than beading or rolling off. Amphiphilic plant-derived surfactant molecules orient at the water–leaf interface, lowering the contact angle and enabling penetration across waxy, hairy, or hydrophobic cuticles. This improves the distribution, adhesion, and cuticle absorption of active ingredients in the spray solution, directly enhancing the efficacy of foliar inputs at the same dose.
